Monte Carlo Convergence Analysis
Figure 2. Monte Carlo convergence analysis showing the cumulative moving average of CAI strength as a function of sample size. The shaded region indicates ±1 standard error of the mean (SEM), which narrows as more samples are drawn. A stable plateau is reached after approximately 2000 samples, with the cumulative mean converging to 448.0 MPa (SEM ≈ 1.2 MPa at n = 3000).
